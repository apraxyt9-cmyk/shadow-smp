WHITELIST ENTRY

Please read our gameplay rules below carefully before submitting your custom whitelisting request.

  • No hacking, client modifications, or X-Ray resource packs allowed.
  • Respect all community members. Griefing or stealing from bases is strictly banned.
  • Do not build close to another player's base without explicit permission.
  • Keep the global game chat friendly and free from spam or toxicity.
  • PvP Policy: PvP fights are only permitted if both involved members mutually agree beforehand. No random killing (RDK).
  • Stealing or killing another player's pets or farm livestock is forbidden.
  • Pranks are allowed, but they must not permanently ruin items, destroy structures, or kill anyone.
